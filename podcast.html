What the podcast is about

In each episode, we take one big theme—like inflation, AI disruption, or new export drives—and break it down into stories, real-world examples, and practical takeaways you can actually use.

Perfect for commutes, workouts, or quiet evenings when you want to understand the world without drowning in jargon.
