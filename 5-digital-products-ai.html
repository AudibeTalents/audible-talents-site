1. E-Books: Write with ChatGPT, Design with Canva for Passive Income

(Check out the full video for a hands-on demo!)

E-books remain one of the easiest digital products to start with. With AI assistance, you can go from idea to published e-book in a day. In fact, some creators note that if you write your ebook with AI, it can be done in less than one day!

  • Brainstorm & Outline: Use ChatGPT to brainstorm profitable e-book topics or niches. Prompt: “Outline an e-book about [your topic]”.
  • Write the content with AI: Expand each chapter with prompts like “Write a detailed section about [chapter topic]”. Refine and add your own examples.
  • Design with Canva: Use templates, create a strong cover, format for readability, and add visuals or AI-generated illustrations if needed.
  • Publish and sell: Export as PDF/ePub and sell via Amazon KDP, your own site, or marketplaces like Gumroad/Etsy. Promote with teasers and snippets.

Pro tip: Ask ChatGPT to write your e-book description, landing page copy, and social captions.

2. Mobile Apps: ChatGPT-Assisted Development and Publishing on Google Play

(Check out the full video to see the app in action and the publishing process.)

Mobile apps are a high-reward digital product. Traditionally, building an app was for engineers only—but not anymore. With ChatGPT and no-code tools, you can develop a simple app and publish it on app stores.

  • Ideation with AI: Ask ChatGPT for app ideas in your niche and validate by browsing Google Play / App Store.
  • Build with no-code / low-code: Use tools like Make.com, Thunkable, Adalo, Glide, Bubble, etc. Ask ChatGPT for logic, formulas, and code snippets.
  • UI/UX design: Use templates and tools like Canva/Uizard for icons, layouts, and simple wireframes.
  • Testing: Run on devices, get feedback, and use ChatGPT for a testing checklist.
  • Publishing & monetizing: Google Play developer account ($25 one-time) + monetization (paid, freemium, ads).

3. AI Agents for Businesses: Sell ChatGPT-Powered Services to Companies

(Check out the full video to see how we created a chatbot from scratch.)

Businesses are eager to automate and streamline tasks using AI. This creates a golden opportunity: you can develop and sell AI agents or chatbots tailored to business needs.

  • Identify a use case: customer support, lead generation, shopping assistants, internal HR Q&A bots, scheduling assistants, etc.
  • Choose tools: no-code bot builders with AI integrations, Make/Zapier + ChatGPT, or frameworks like LangChain if you code.
  • Train/configure: define role, provide business context (FAQs/knowledge base), create example Q&A with ChatGPT.
  • Test scenarios: simulate real requests and refine prompts/training data for accuracy and helpfulness.
  • Sell: setup fees + maintenance, subscriptions, templates, niche marketplace listings.

5. YouTube Video Summaries & Affiliate Blogs: Turn Content into Cash with AI

(Check out the full video to see a live example of creating and monetizing a video summary.)

Here’s an unconventional but powerful strategy: use AI to turn existing content (YouTube videos, trending articles) into monetized summaries and blog posts. Many people want quick takeaways without watching 15–20 minutes of video.

  • Summarize with AI: paste transcripts into ChatGPT and ask for structured summaries.
  • Use tools like Sider AI: browser extensions can pull transcripts and generate summaries quickly.
  • Build affiliate articles: add helpful, relevant affiliate links where they fit naturally.
  • Monetize: ads (later), affiliates, sponsorships, or premium “summary packs”.
